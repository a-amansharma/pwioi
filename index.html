Frequently Asked Questions

Why should one join PW IOI?

Our courses redefine learning, encouraging innovative thinking for the modern professional.

Do you offer fully residential programs?

Yes, we offer fully residential programs in the vibrant city of Bangalore.

Do you provide placement assistance?

We provide abundant placement assistance and opportunities to expand your professional network.

What's the eligibility criteria for direct admission?

Direct admission is based on scores from the following exams:

JEE Main: 95%ile and above
NEET: 710 marks and above
CUET: 580 marks and above
IPMAT: 400 marks and above

OR

For SOM: Students with 70% or above in both Class 10th and 12th are eligible.
For SOT: Students with 75% or above in both Class 10th and 12th are eligible.

Why is this program in-person and not online?

Our in-person program offers personalized experiences within a vibrant student community, making you industry-ready.
