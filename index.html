Frequently Asked Questions

Why one should Join PW IOI?

Our courses redefine learning, encouraging innovative thinking for the modern professional.

Do you offer fully residential programs?

Yes, fully residential programs in the vibrant city of Bangalore.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Abundant placement assistance and a chance to expand your professional network.

What's the eligibility criteria for direct admission?

Direct admission criteria depends on your score of below competitive exam.
JEE Main : 95%tile and above
NEET : 710 Marks and above
CUET : 580 marks and above
IPMAT : 400 marks and above

OR

FOR SOM : Students who have achieved a score of 70% or above in both Class 10th and 12th are also eligible for direct admission.

FOR SOT : Students who have achieved a score of 75% or above in both Class 10th and 12th are also eligible for direct admission.

Why is this program in-person and not online?

Personalized experiences within a vibrant student community to make you industry-ready.
